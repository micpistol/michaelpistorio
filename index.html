About

I'm a product strategist exploring how foundational AI models can transform VFX production workflows—from hands-on research to industry validation.

After 8+ years managing VFX production at Netflix (5,000+ shots, $20M+ in vendor spend), I identified a persistent inefficiency: 30-40% of prep budgets go to manual, repetitive work with minimal creative value. I founded Next Wave Intelligence to investigate how intelligent tooling can augment these workflows without disrupting artist autonomy.

Current Research: Next Wave Intelligence

Next Wave Intelligence is my research initiative exploring AI-native infrastructure for VFX prep workflows.

Focus Areas

  • Promptable segmentation for tracked roto (SAM/SAM2)
  • Depth inference for editorial and compositing workflows
  • Automated QC diagnostics for plate readiness
  • Pipeline-native integration (Flow, Deadline, ShotGrid)

Approach

Infrastructure-first. Modular. Compatible with existing studio ecosystems. Artist-augmentative, not artist-replacing.

Current Status

Conducting industry validation through conversations with VFX supervisors, pipeline TDs, and studio technology leads. Built 3 proof-of-concept demonstrations. Exploring pilot partnerships for production deployment.
