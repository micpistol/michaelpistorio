About

I'm a product strategist and researcher exploring how foundational AI models can transform VFX production workflows‚Äîfrom hands-on prototyping to industry validation.

After 8+ years managing VFX production at Netflix (5,000+ shots, $20M+ vendor spend), I identified a persistent inefficiency: 30-40% of prep budgets go to manual, repetitive work with minimal creative value.

I founded Next Wave Intelligence to investigate how intelligent tooling can augment these workflows without disrupting artist autonomy. My approach combines Netflix-class production experience with AI research training (UCLA AI certification, deep learning coursework).

Current focus: production-aware segmentation (SAM2), depth inference, neural rendering integration, and building the cognitive supply chain that makes VFX pipelines learn from production data.
