About

Background

I spent 8+ years managing VFX production at Netflix, overseeing 5,000+ shots and $20M+ in vendor spend across series like The Umbrella Academy, Locke & Key, and other Netflix Originals.

Through that experience, I identified a core inefficiency: 30-40% of VFX prep budgets go to manual, repetitive tasks with minimal creative value. This sparked my research into AI-native infrastructure for creative pipelines.

Current Focus

Founded Next Wave Intelligence, an R&D practice exploring how AI functions as production infrastructure—not a plugin. Current research areas include neural rendering integration, promptable segmentation, and building cognitive supply chains that learn from production data.

Completing UCLA AI certification while prototyping tools and conducting industry validation with VFX supervisors, pipeline TDs, and studio technology leads.

Open to product roles, research partnerships, and pilot collaborations
